Why VPathRewards Drives the Behavior Advertisers Pay For

Most cashback publishers compete for the same one-time bargain hunters. VPathRewards is engineered differently: every transaction increases a member's lifetime platform spend, which unlocks higher commission shares (Bronze 20% → Silver 35% → Gold 50% → Platinum 65% → Diamond 80% → Emerald 85% → Sapphire 90% → Ruby 95% → Obsidian 100%). The result is a structural incentive for members to return through our links rather than direct, search, or competing publishers. For advertisers, this translates into higher repeat purchase rates, longer customer lifecycles, and improved blended ROAS over time — without paywalls, subscriptions, or aggressive coupon stacking.

Repeat-Purchase Engine

Members earn more by spending more on the platform. The 9-tier ladder (Bronze → Obsidian) converts one-time shoppers into habitual returners — the LTV behavior advertisers prize most.

Quality Traffic Filter

No paywall, no incentivized clicks, no cookie stuffing. Users arrive with confirmed purchase intent. Conversion rates favor cashback-first traffic over coupon-aggregator traffic.

Clean Attribution

CJ deep-link compatible with encoded SID/sub-ID per member and placement. First-party tracking layered with CJ pixel/postback. Cashback locks only after CJ validation.

Member Tier Structure

All tiers are free. Members advance through verified lifetime spend on the platform. Percentages shown are the member's share of the merchant commission earned per qualifying transaction, after a flat $5 platform service fee is deducted. Conversions where the merchant commission is $5 or less are exempt from the fee — the member receives the full commission.
