## 3. Product Overview

**What it is:** A cashback/rewards aggregator. Members shop through VPath
Rewards' affiliate links and earn a share of the commission back as cash.

**How it works:**
1. **Sign up free** — create an account in seconds; no credit card required.
2. **Shop** — browse offers and click through to a merchant via an affiliate link.
3. **Get cashback** — earn money back on every qualifying purchase, and withdraw anytime.

**Categories:** Travel · Shopping · Food · Health · Home · Entertainment

**Affiliate network:** Purchases are tracked through CJ Affiliate.

**Withdrawals:**
- Minimum withdrawal: **$10.00**
- Processing time: **5–10 business days**
- Identity is verified before processing

**Referrals:** Members get a unique referral link and earn bonuses when
referred users complete qualifying actions.

## 4. Membership Tiers

VPath Rewards is **free for everyone** — there are no paid plans. A member's
tier sets the **share of the affiliate commission they keep** on each
confirmed purchase. Members climb tiers automatically as their lifetime
confirmed spend grows.

| Tier | Unlocks at (lifetime confirmed spend) | Member keeps |
|------|----------------------------------------|--------------|
| Bronze | $0 (default) | 20% |
| Silver | $500 | 35% |
| Gold | $1,500 | 50% |
| Platinum | $3,000 | 65% |
| Diamond | $6,000 | 80% |
| Emerald | $12,000 | 85% |
| Sapphire | $24,000 | 90% |
| Ruby | $48,000 | 95% |
| Obsidian | $96,000 | 100% |

> The member's cashback on a purchase is `commission_earned × tier share`,
> after a flat $5 platform fee (waived for small commissions).
